## Client Complaints Section

The **Client Complaints** section is a dedicated module within the **Clients** area of the system that enables clients, administrators, and support teams to record, track, manage, and resolve customer complaints efficiently. This section helps improve customer satisfaction, accountability, transparency, and service quality by ensuring that every complaint is documented and followed through to resolution.

---

# Purpose of the Client Complaints Section

The main purpose of this section is to:

* Capture complaints raised by clients.
* Track complaint progress from submission to resolution.
* Improve communication between clients and support teams.
* Maintain records for accountability and future reference.
* Analyze recurring issues and improve service delivery.
* Ensure timely response and resolution of customer concerns.

---

# Key Features of the Client Complaints Module

Allows staff or clients to submit a complaint into the system.

### Information Captured

* Complaint ID / Reference Number
* Client Name
* Client Account Number
* Contact Information

* Complaint Priority Level

### Example Complaint Categories

* Poor Service
* Delayed Delivery

# Sample Workflow

1. Client submits a complaint.
2. System generates a complaint reference number.
3. Complaint is assigned to a department.
4. Staff reviews and responds.
5. Complaint status changes to “In Progress.”
6. Resolution is provided.
7. Client confirms satisfaction.
8. Complaint is marked as “Closed.”
